Serial Comm Interface Preventive Maintenance. Preventive maintenance of the serial communication interface consists of keeping the serial communication circuit, port, cable, and device clean and free of dust. Vacuum dust off the serial communication board (or circuit on the Ile motherboard) periodically. Check the board's edge connector for oxidation. Make sure board is seated securely in its slot. Check the serial communication interface port and cable for oxidation, and clean it off if present. Make sure the cable is firmly connected.

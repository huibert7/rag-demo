Running the Disk Drive Diagnostic Module. Follow the instructions provided in "Running the Diagnostic Program" in Chapter 2 to run the Disk Drive Diagnostic module. Then press D to initiate the Disk Drive Diagnostic module. Remove the System Diagnostic Program diskette and insert a blank, formatted diskette to be used for the test. This diagnostic checks the disk drive and media. It also tests the two main functions of the disk drive: reading from and writing to a disk. It exercises the entire disk, checking for many types of problems. If a problem is encountered, an error message will displayed on the screen. These error messages are referenced in the "Disk Drive Troubleshooting and Repair Guidelines" section above, and list remedies and responses to the error.
